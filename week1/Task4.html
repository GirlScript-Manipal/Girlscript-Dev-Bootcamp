GirlScript Winter Mentorship Bootcamp

About:

GirlScript aims to become world's biggest tech community for learning technical skills and getting opportunities around the globe. Our Mission is to empower people and specially under represented groups by educating them on technology and related necessary skills. To create local opportunity, growth and impact via tech education in every country around the world and to bring diversity in tech domain. GirlScript is the fastest growing tech-community in India. It is a non-profit project brought to you by GirlScript Foundation to help beginners in technology.
