About Girlscript

Girlscript is a non-profit organisation that was established 2017. Girlscript is working to change lives by imparting tech education and relevant skills. Girlscript aims at promoting equality in tech and helping beginners take their first steps in technology.

Our Mission:

To empower people and specially under represented groups by educating them on technology and related necessary skills. To create local opportunity, growth and impact via tech education in every country around the world and to bring diversity in tech domain.

Our Goal:

  1. To educate people in tech
  2. Remove myths about tech education
  3. Spread awareness about opportunities in tier two & tier three cities
  4. Introduce tech content that is helpful for everyone
