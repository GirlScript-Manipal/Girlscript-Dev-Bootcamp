About the Bootcamp

As the year is coming to an end, we inch closer to the The GirlScript Winter Mentorship Bootcamp. The Girlscript Bootcamp is a place where you will be able to master skills in whichever domain you choose . The bootcamp will be for 6 weeks, you can choose 2 domains out of the 4 listed domains:

  • Competitive Programming
  • Web Development
  • Graphic Design
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
The domains you choose will be taught to you in a duration of 6 weeks taking your skill levels from novice to intermediate. Giving you the right platform to start your journey in the field of technology with the right skills.
