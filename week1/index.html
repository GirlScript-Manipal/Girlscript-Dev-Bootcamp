Girlscript Winter Mentorship Bootcamp

About

As we near the end of winter, we inch closer to GirlScript's much awaited event of the year, the WINTER MENTORSHIP! We welcome you to 6 whole weeks of bountiful learning starting from 1st February, 2022. We have domain bootcamps that will help master all skills and all you have to do is follow along. We will provide you with an incredibly well curated resource roadmap, along with interactive doubt clarification sessions, workshops and capstone projects. So, what are you waiting for. Strap into your favourite hand-knit sweater and sign up asap to create a supportive and skilled community!
