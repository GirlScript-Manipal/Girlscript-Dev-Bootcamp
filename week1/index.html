About us

GirlScript aims to become the world's biggerst tech community for learning techincal skills and getting opportunities around the globe. Our mission is to empower people especially underrepresented groups by providing techincal education. We are focussed on bringing about diversity in the tech domain.

GirlScript Winter Mentorship

The Winter Mentorship Programme will provide 6 weeks of learning and training in 6 technical domains, namely AI/ML, Web/App Development, Competitive Programming, Design and Robotics.

Resources will be provided and there will be weekly workshops as well as doubt clearing sessions. This programme is open to all and will start from the 1st February, 2022.
