GIRLSCRIPT WINTER MENTORSHIP BOOTCAMP

About GirlScript

Girlscript is a non-profit organisation that is working to change lives by imparting tech education and relevant skills.
Girlscript aims at promoting equality in tech and helping beginners take their first steps in technology.

About Winter Mentorship Bootcamp

As we near the end of winter, we inch closer to GirlScript's much awaited event of the year, the WINTER MENTORSHIP! We welcome you to 6 whole weeks of bountiful learning starting from 1st February, 2022. We have domain bootcamps that will help master all skills and all you have to do is follow along.
We will provide you with an incredibly well curated resource roadmap, along with interactive doubt clarification sessions, workshops and capstone projects.

We bring to you a one-on-one mentorship program in the following tracks:

  1. Machine Learning (Python)
  2. Computer Vision (Python)
  3. Web development
  4. Competitive coding ( DS, Algorithms and Interview tips )
  5. Reinforcement Learning in Robotics
  6. App Development
  7. Business Management
  8. Graphic Design
