About Us

Girlscript is a non-profit organisation that is working to change lives by imparting tech education and relevant skills. Girlscript aims at promoting equality in tech and helping beginners take their first steps in technology.
we're so very excited to introduce you to the various domains of tech and be your guide-posts as you navigate your way around the different niches. We're inviting people of all gender identities in Manipal to come be a part of a supportive and uplifting community where we learn from one and all. The enthusiastic team behind GirlScript Manipal has oh-so-many wonderful things planned and we can't wait to present them to the world! Stay tuned and watch out for so much more :) Stay connected with us on our Social media handles to keep an eye on all the latest updates and events.
